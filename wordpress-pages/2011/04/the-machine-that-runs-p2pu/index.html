The Machine that runs P2PU

by P

I posted an update on the administrative underbelly of P2PU over at http://blogs.p2pu.org/blog/2011/04/04/p2pu-the-machine/. It covers a lot of the important (but slightly mundane) details of incorporating as a non-profit organization. While it’s annoying to fill out endless forms, many of our users are curious about how things work behind the scenes (surprisingly they work just like in front of the scenes) and it made sense to provide a big comprehensive overview of where we are and what is happening. I added some nice pictures to help readers stay awake.
