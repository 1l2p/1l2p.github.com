One thing you can try with BubbleTimer is to use the notes feature to put in what meetings you went to. There is a sticky pad note at the end (right side) of every activity near the time total for that activity. Just click it and you have a free form spot for notes. Later, if you look at the print summary of how you spent your time each day, those notes are shown. So you’ll be able to see where your meeting time went.
