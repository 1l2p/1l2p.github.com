J. Philipp Schmidt

I am curious about most things, and passionate about learning. I create new tools, platforms, and institutions that spur creative learning. My core design principle is generosity.

Short bio

J. Philipp Schmidt is Director of Learning Innovation at the MIT Media Lab, where he leads the ML Learning initiative, teaches courses, and conducts research on learning communities. He is also a cofounder and board member of Peer 2 Peer University (P2PU), a non-profit organization that provides access to online higher education through public libraries. Philipp served on the founding board of the OpenCourseWare Consortium and co-authored the Cape Town Open Education Declaration. He has received Shuttleworth and Ashoka and MIT Media Lab Director's fellowships.

Philipp lives in Boston and sometimes misses Cape Town and Cologne.
