J. Philipp Schmidt

I create tools, platforms, and institutions for learning. My core design principle is generosity.

I am based at the MIT Media Lab, where I work on learning innovation (MLx, Unhangouts, Learning Creative Learning). I am also a cofounder of Peer 2 Peer University (P2PU), a non-profit organization and online community that experiments with new ways of learning. I served on the founding board of the OpenCourseWare Consortium and co-authored the Cape Town Open Education Declaration.

I live in Boston and miss Cape Town.
