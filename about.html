J. Philipp Schmidt

I am curious about most things, and passionate about learning. I create new tools, platforms, and institutions that spur creative learning. My core design principle is generosity.

I am Director of Learning Innovation at the MIT Media Lab, where I lead the Learning Learning initiative, teach courses, and conduct research. I am also a cofounder and board member of Peer 2 Peer University (P2PU), a non-profit organization and online community that experiments with new ways of learning. I served on the founding board of the OpenCourseWare Consortium and co-authored the Cape Town Open Education Declaration. I received Shuttleworth and Ashoka and MIT Media Lab Director's fellowships.

I live in Boston and miss Cape Town and Cologne.
